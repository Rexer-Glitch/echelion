About Echelon International

At Echelon International, we aim to provide you with a transparent, simple and hassle free application process. Whether you are going to your very first university, pursuing your masters, phD or even just taking a gap year to figure things out, Echelon has you covered.

We believe that education is priceless so we partner with the best schools to offers you just that. Our full-service experience includes application process & follow ups, scholarship matching, interview arrangements, flight booking, China arrival briefings as well as arrange pick ups for your respective schools.

Key differentiators:

  • Personalized counseling
  • Comprehensive admission support
  • Up to 100% tuition coverage
  • Visa and scholarship assistance
